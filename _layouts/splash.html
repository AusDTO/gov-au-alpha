Building GOV.AU

The process

The mission was to build the GOV.AU Alpha to prove people are able to use government services without having to know anything about how government works.

How we did it

This prototype focuses on a single task around starting a business, which eventually needs to hire overseas staff. After the user answers a few basic questions upfront, the prototype then matches the right government information(local, state or federal) to their situation. Checklists are provided for them to consider and with links to explore further or start the process. Additionally, several Ministers’ and Department pages have been built to illustrate how different types of content could be rendered.

That’s why the Digital Transformation Office (or DTO) has been created - to lead the government in transforming our services to improve your experience.
