Do you need to register a business?

You don’t have to register a business if you are:

  • Pursuing a hobby

    For taxation purposes, you are pursuing a hobby if you:

    • Do not engage in an activity for commercial reasons
    • Do not intend to make a profit
    • Do not regularly and repeatedly engage in an activity
    • Do not plan, organise and carry out your hobby in a business-like way
  • An apprentice
  • A trainee
  • An employee who is not a director or associate of your  proposed business

    A director is a member of a company who is elected or appointed to direct or manage that company.

    A colleague, partner or fellow employee who is typically more junior than the Company Director.

Do any of these describe you?
